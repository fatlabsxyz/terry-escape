HOW TO PLAY TERRY ESCAPE

OBJECTIVE

Be the last player with living agents on the board!

GAME RULES

  • SETUP: Each player deploys 4 agents in their corner zone
  • TURNS: Players take turns being "active" - only the active player can act
  • ACTIONS: On your turn, you MUST either:
    • MOVE: Move one agent to an adjacent cell (up/down/left/right)
    • TRAP: Place a trap in an adjacent cell from one of your agents

ZERO-KNOWLEDGE PROOFS

This game uses ZK proofs to ensure fair play without revealing positions to other players. When you see "Generating Proof", the game is cryptographically securing your move!

STRATEGY TIPS

  • Spread your agents to avoid chain eliminations
  • Use traps to control key positions
  • Force opponents into bad positions
  • Remember: The active player has advantage in collisions!
